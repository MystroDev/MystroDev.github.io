Partnerships

Consortium CIPROMED

As part of the CIPROMED consortium, we work with 17 partners representing ten Euro-Mediterranean countries including eight industrial partners and eight research partners, including universities as well as internationally recognized research institutes. CIPROMED brings together different experts from different fields, from biotechnology, agriculture, aquaculture, food engineering and food production, working together to explore and exploit the results obtained in the Mediterranean region and beyond .

Missions

The Green Green Development and Innovation association works in the following areas:

Sustainable agricultural development

We work towards the development of sustainable agriculture on a local, regional and national scale, exploring various areas such as ecology, technology and renewable energy. Our participatory and inclusive approach with local stakeholders is based on the adoption of modern, innovative and environmentally friendly technologies.

Protection of the environment and ecology

Our commitment focuses on preserving the environment, safeguarding natural resources and protecting biodiversity in the face of ecological, anthropogenic and climatic threats.

Economic development

Our association plays an active and committed role in promoting economic development, particularly in rural areas where it is of crucial importance. We work to encourage structuring projects which not only stimulate local economic activity but also contribute to environmental and social sustainability. This includes supporting farmers to adopt innovative and environmentally friendly agricultural practices, as well as promoting local and sustainable supply chains. We seek to create employment opportunities, strengthen local skills and encourage the involvement of young people and women in sustainable economic initiatives.

Research and Development

We carry out research activities and experimental trials in collaboration with research institutions and universities nationally and internationally through the involvement of trainees and doctoral students in different subjects. Our research activities focus on crucial sectors such as agronomy, agri-food, plant and animal production, ecology and the environment. Our research activities aim to explore new innovations to enhance sustainability and productivity in these areas. /p>

Communication, dissemination, awareness and training

We actively contribute to raising awareness and training individuals and organizations who share our goals. Our commitment includes strengthening the skills of farmers, associations and cooperatives at local, regional and international levels. As part of. Our communication and dissemination activities, we organize conferences, forums, seminars and publish research and periodicals in national and international newspapers and journals.

Partnership and cooperation

We promote coordination and cooperation with various national and international organizations, institutes and industrialists. The exchange of experiences and the implementation of joint activities within the framework of cooperation projects constitute our contribution to the development of large-scale positioning policies.

Presentation

Presentation _

The Green Development and Innovation Association (GDI) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization working in the development of sustainable and ecological agriculture, the preservation of the environment, the protection of natural resources and biodiversity in the face of dangers likely to cause environmental or ecological imbalances, in the current context of climate change. The contributes to sustainable development at local, regional and national levels in various agricultural, environmental and renewable energy fields, by adopting renewable approaches, modern, innovative and environmentally friendly technologies. Its objective is to contribute to the promotion of economic, social and environmental development, particularly in rural areas. The association also contributes to raising awareness of issues related to the environment, ecology, agriculture and sustainable development by involving young people and giving particular importance to the role of women in sustainable development.

Our projects

CIPROMED Project Challenge Solution for well-being.

The CIPROMED project (Circular and Inclusive utilization of alternative PROteins in the MEDiterranean value chains) is part of the association's international cooperation activities in the field of Research and Development which aims to support the association's position in the Mediterranean region. The project includes 17 partners (the Green Development and Innovation association is a partner) from ten Mediterranean countries including eight industrial partners and eight Research and Development partners. The CIPROMED project aims to: - Implement a circular approach model for the use of alternative protein sources for the Mediterranean agri-food system. - Increase the stability and resilience of Mediterranean agri-food production systems through the appropriate and direct exploitation of traditional crops produced locally

The agricultural test field of the pilot unit of the Green Development and Innovation association

Launch of field trials to test the effectiveness of insect frass and chicken manure in the bio-fertilization of crops On December 26, 2023, the association launched experiments to evaluate the beneficial effect of soldier fly frass black and chicken manure in soil bio-fertilization, plant growth and crop productivity. These tests are part of the two projects CIPROMED and ADVAGROMED in collaboration with the startup EntomoNutris. The trials were carried out on beans and barley while waiting to expand other experiments on the cultivation of lupine and onions.

Organisation du congrès international “Agroecology, a new approach for sustainable and resilient food systems”

The Green Development and Innovation association, the 3BIO laboratory of the Khénifra Higher School of Technology, the University of Turin, the University of Thessaly organized the international congress entitled “Agroecology, a new approach for sustainable and resilient food systems » which was held on December 21 and 22, 2023 in Khenifra. The congress was an opportunity to exchange with several expert speakers, researchers, academics, doctoral students, industrialists and decision-makers in a scientific and collective debate to follow the transition and progress of agroecological practices and technologies fully aligned with current practices. contemporary aspects of the circular economy. The congress was attended by the vice-president of the Sultan Moulay Slimane University and the director of the Khénifra Higher School of Technology.

Organisation du congrès international “Agroecology, a new approach for sustainable and resilient food systems”

L’association Green Development and Innovation, le laboratoire 3BIO de l'École Supérieure de Technologie de Khénifra, l'Université de Turin, l'Université de Thessalie ont organisé le congrès international intitulé “Agroecology, a new approach for sustainable and resilient food systems » qui s’est tenue les jours 21 et 22 décembre 2023 à Khenifra. Le congrès était l’occasion pour échanger avec plusieurs experts conférenciers, des chercheurs, des universitaires, des doctorants, des industriels et des décideurs dans un débat scientifique et collectif pour suivre la transition et les progrès des pratiques et technologies agroécologiques pleinement alignées sur les pratiques contemporaines de l'économie circulaire. Le congrès a connu la présence de Mme la vice-président de l’Université Sultan Moulay Slimane et Mr. le directeur de l’École Supérieure de Technologie de Khénifra.

Cette rencontre était l’occasion pour visiter les différents unités de recherche et laboratoires au sein de l’Université de Thessalie.

Visite des partenaires du projet CIPROMED

Le 23 et 24 novembre 2023, l’association a connu la visite de nos partenaires grecques, italiens et allemands dans le cadres des activités d’échanges multilatérales entre les partenaires du projet CIPROMED. La visite était l’occasion pour discuter les avancées des recherches menées par notre association dans le cadre du projet. Cette visite était l’occasion pour visiter l’unité pilote d’élevage et de production des insectes de la mouche soldat noire à Ain Taoujdate, installé dans le cadre du projet ADVAGROMED, coordonné au Maroc par l’Université Sultan Moulay Slimane.

Le champ d'essais agricoles de l’unité pilote de l’association Green Development and Innovation

Lancement des essaies de terrain pour tester l’efficacité du frass des insectes et le fumier des poulets dans la bio-fertilisation des cultures Le 26 décembre 2023, l’association a lancé les expérimentations pour évaluer l’effet bénéfique du frass de la mouche soldat noire et le fumier des poulets dans la bio-fertilisation du sol, la croissance des plantes et la productivité des cultures. Ces essaies s’inscrit dans le cadre des deux projets CIPROMED et ADVAGROMED en collaboration avec la startup EntomoNutris. Les essaies se sont menées sur la fève et l’orge en attendant d’élargir d’autres expérimentations sur la culture du lupin et de l’oignon.

Le Sommet arabe de l'entrepreneuriat

The president and advisor of the Green Development and Innovation association participated in the Arab Summit of SMEs which was held in Marrakech during the days of December 12 to 14, 2023. The Summit constituted a regional platform aimed at empowering small and medium-sized businesses in the Arab region, to improve their performance and expand their potential beyond borders. The participation of our association comes as part of the communication and dissemination actions of our activities carried out within the framework of the CIPROMED project. The members of the association had the opportunity to meet and discuss with the director of the PRIMA foundation, the financing program and donor of the CIPROMED project.

Khenifra Congress

On December 26, 2023, the association launched experiments to evaluate the beneficial effect of black soldier fly frass and chicken manure in soil bio-fertilization, plant growth and crop productivity. These tests are part of the two projects CIPROMED and ADVAGROMED in collaboration with the startup EntomoNutris. The trials were carried out on beans and barley while waiting to expand other experiments on the cultivation of lupine and onions.
